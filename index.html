Frequently Asked Questions

How does Origin Scout determine the country?

We analyze product descriptions, technical specifications, and seller metadata. Our scoring engine then assigns a "Confidence Level" based on the clarity and consistency of that data.

Is my browsing data private?

Yes. Origin Scout processes all product page data locally on your device. We do not track your identity or sell your browsing history.
